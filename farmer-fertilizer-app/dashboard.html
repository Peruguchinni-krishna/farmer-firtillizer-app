About this app

This tool helps farmers choose the right fertilizer based on soil nutrients and weather conditions.

Fertilizer guide

  • Urea - Nitrogen rich fertilizer for leafy growth.
  • DAP - Phosphorus rich fertilizer for strong roots.
  • MOP - Potassium rich fertilizer for plant immunity.
  • Balanced NPK - Use when N, P, and K are all adequate.
